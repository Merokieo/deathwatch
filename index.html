About the Deathwatch

The Deathwatch are the elite of the elite — chosen from all Chapters of the Adeptus Astartes to serve the Ordo Xenos in their eternal vigil against the alien menace. Bound by duty, honor, and brotherhood, they are the Emperor's wrath incarnate.

Combat Doctrine

Each Kill-Team is forged for a purpose — surgical, precise, and devastating. Missions vary from deep-strike eliminations to stealth recon operations. Adaptability and relentless execution define our tactics.

Armory
